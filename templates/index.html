Frequently Asked Questions

Some questions you may have...

  • What is Phishing?

    Phishing is a type of online scam where attackers try to trick individuals into providing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card numbers, or personal details by pretending to be a trustworthy entity.

  • Why should I care about phishing?

    Knowing about phishing is important because it helps you identify and avoid potential scams, protecting your personal information and financial assets.

  • How can I protect Myself from Phishing attacks

    You can protect yourself from phishing attacks by being cautious of unsolicited communications, verifying the legitimacy of requests for information, and using security software and two-factor authentication.
